Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about planning your dream vacation with me

Getting Started

My planning services are complimentary! I'm compensated by the travel suppliers (hotels, cruise lines, etc.) when you book through me, so there's no additional cost to you. You get expert planning at no extra charge.

I handle everything from start to finish: resort/hotel recommendations, dining reservations, park strategies, cruise cabin selection, shore excursion planning, and day-by-day itineraries. I'm also available throughout your trip for questions or if anything needs to be adjusted.

I'll review your request and reach out within 24-48 hours to discuss your dream vacation in detail. We'll have a conversation about your preferences, budget, and must-haves, then I'll create a personalized proposal for you.

Booking & Timing

For Disney vacations, I recommend booking 6-12 months in advance, especially for peak seasons. Cruises often have the best selection 12-18 months out. However, I can also help with shorter-notice trips when availability allows.

Life happens, and I'm here to help navigate changes. Each booking has its own cancellation policy, which I'll explain upfront. I always recommend travel insurance for added peace of mind, and I can help you find the right coverage for your trip.

Absolutely! I strongly recommend travel insurance for all trips. I can help you understand your options and find a policy that fits your needs and budget. It's a small investment for significant peace of mind.

During Your Trip

I'm available via text, email, or phone throughout your vacation. Whether you need a last-minute dining reservation, have a question about your itinerary, or just want to share how much fun you're having, I'm here for you!
